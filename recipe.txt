Guacamole

2 avocados
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. chopped tomato
1 clove minced garlic
2 tbsp chopped cilantro
1/2 jalapeño, seeded and diced
juice of 1/2 lime
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper

Combine the avocado, onion, tomato, garlic, cilantro, and jalapeño, mixing until avocado is smooth.  Season with salt and pepper.  Top with the lime juice.  Serve with chips.
